Our Services

  • Transformer/Transporter booking
  • Optimized truck loading using predictive models

Cost Models

For trips under 100 km and weight under 15 tons: Cost = 10 * distance * weight

For trips under 200 km and weight under 30 tons: Cost = 7 * distance * weight

For trips over 200 km: Cost = 4 * distance * weight
